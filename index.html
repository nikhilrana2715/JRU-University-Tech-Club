About Our Club

Our Story

Founded in 2010, the University Tech Club has grown from a small group of computer science enthusiasts to a vibrant community of over 500 members from all academic disciplines.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical technology skills, preparing students for the rapidly evolving tech industry.

Our Journey

2010 - Humble Beginnings

Started with 12 members meeting weekly in a small classroom

2014 - Rapid Growth

Membership grew to 150+ with university recognition

2018 - National Recognition

Won 3 national hackathons and established industry partnerships

Our Mission

To empower students with technical skills, foster innovation, and build a community that supports learning and professional growth in technology fields.
